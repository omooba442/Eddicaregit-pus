Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to Eddicare Foundation?

Donations can be made online via our website. We accept various forms of payment including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal.

Eddicare Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving health , Youth Empowerment, widows empowerment and education access and quality for underprivileged communities.

We offer Youth Empowerment, widows empowerment, medical outreach, improving the life of less privileged persons and scholarship programs, teacher training workshops, educational resources distribution, and community outreach initiatives.

Scholarship applications can be submitted through our website’s scholarship portal. Detailed guidelines and eligibility criteria are provided there.

You can contact us via email, phone, or through our contact form on the website. Our contact details are available on the "Contact Us" page..

Donations are used to fund our various programs, including scholarships, educational resources, and community projects. Detailed financial reports are available on our transparency page.
