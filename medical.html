EddiCare Medical Outreach

The Eddicare Foundation recently conducted a comprehensive medical outreach in the Ohimini Local Government Area of Benue State, targeting the prevalent health issues of malaria, typhoid, and meningitis. This initiative was designed to provide essential medical care and treatment to underserved rural communities, ensuring that residents received the necessary health interventions and education to combat these diseases.During the outreach, a team of dedicated healthcare professionals provided free medical consultations, diagnostic services, and treatment.

Community members benefited from the distribution of medications and preventive measures, such as mosquito nets, to reduce the incidence of malaria. The program also included awareness sessions to educate the community on the symptoms, prevention, and management of typhoid and meningitis. The Eddicare Foundation’s efforts have significantly impacted the health and well-being of the residents of Ohimini, demonstrating a commitment to improving healthcare access in rural areas. This outreach is a testament to the foundation's mission to address critical health challenges and enhance the quality of life for all.
