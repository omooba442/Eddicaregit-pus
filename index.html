Get To know US

Empowering Lives Through Education and Healthcare

EddiCare Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to improving the lives of underprivileged children in developing nations through access to quality education and healthcare services. Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate individuals, EddiCare has since grown into a global network of volunteers, donors, and partners dedicated to making a difference in the lives of children in need.

  • Education Programs
  • Healthcare Initiatives
  • Provision Of Resources
  • Advocacy and Awareness

Together, we can make a difference in the lives of underprivileged children around the world. Join us in our mission to empower, educate, and uplift those who need it most.

Education Programmes

EddiCare Foundation operates a variety of education programs aimed at providing children with access to quality education. These include:

  • Schools

    Building and renovating schools in underserved communities.

  • Scholarships

    Providing scholarships and educational materials to students in need..

  • Vocations & Initiatives

    Offering vocational training and skills development programs for older children.

    Through these initiatives, we aim to equip children with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in school and beyond.

Can I partner with Eddicare Foundation?

Yes, we are open to partnerships with other organizations and institutions. Please reach out to us through our partnership inquiry form for more details.
