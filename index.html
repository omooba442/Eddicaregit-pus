Empowering Lives Through Education and Healthcare

EddiCare Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to improving the lives of underprivileged children in developing nations through access to quality education and healthcare services. Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate individuals, EddiCare has since grown into a global network of volunteers, donors, and partners dedicated to making a difference in the lives of children in need.
