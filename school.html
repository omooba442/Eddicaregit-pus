Eureka School Competition

The Eureka School Competition, organized by the Kaduna State Education Authority Cluster A Kujama and sponsored by Eddicare Foundation, was a resounding success. Both the organizers and participants, including teachers and students, were filled with joy and excitement throughout the event.Eddicare Foundation generously provided all the gift items for various activities, including football, creative arts, track and field events, and cultural showcases. Their support and commitment to fostering education and extracurricular activities led to Eddicare Foundation receiving an Award of Excellence in Service to Humanity.
